Week 01

What went well

This week I accomplished all my goals. I picked my topic, finished research and conducted 5 interviews, with another interview happening this evening. What can be improved

My problem statement and insight could be improved or narrowed down a bit more.

What actions will I take for the next sprint.

Next week, I'll ask for feedback on my probelm statement and insight, then refine them.
